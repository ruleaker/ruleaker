### About

### Areas of work

#### Offensive Security
- External → internal full-chain (web exploitation → AD takeover → persistence)
- Loader development & EDR evasion: direct syscalls, sleep encryption, module stomping, AMSI / ETW bypass
- Reverse engineering: binary triage, vulnerability research, malware analysis
- CTF: pwn / rev, on and off

#### On-chain & Crypto
- CEX systematic trading: perps funding-rate arb, basis, market making (Binance / OKX / Bybit)
- DeFi engineering with Foundry: audits, flash-loan composition, account abstraction
- MEV: mempool monitoring, sandwich, backrun, cross-venue DEX / CEX arbitrage

#### Macro & Markets
- Liquidity framework: M2 · DXY · RRP · TGA · global central-bank balance sheets
- Cycles: Howard Marks · Dalio long-term debt cycle · Minsky moment
- Tail risk & fat-tail statistics: Taleb's complete Incerto (Antifragile, Black Swan, Fooled by Randomness) · Mandelbrot · Soros reflexivity

#### Philosophy

- Eastern thought: 老子 · 朱熹 · 王阳明
